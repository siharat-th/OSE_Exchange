\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{class_risk_limits}{Risk\+Limits} class represents the risk limits for a particular trading account. 

The \doxylink{class_risk_limits}{Risk\+Limits} class provides methods to update the risk limits, set the log level, enable/disable the risk limits, check various risk conditions, and perform other operations related to risk management.
